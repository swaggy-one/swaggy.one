Walter Clements;

The joke discord bot with no purpose that likes fire trucks and moster trucks.


Walter Clements, based on the meme, is a Discord bot with no functionality besides simply being there just to entertain people. Walter replies when someone says "walter", "i like fire trucks", "i like moster trucks", and also has a chance to say "i like fire trucks and moster trucks" like in the meme. He also has a bunch of joke commands of many categories. A full list of commands can be found by typing "/help" or "w!help" if you have application commands disabled. Walter is currently being heavily updated, with more joke commands coming soon!
